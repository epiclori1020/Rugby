# Rugby S&C Research Synthesis

## Goal

Turn research into usable planning decisions for Rugby Donau.

## Inputs

- Raw Deep Research text.
- Existing files in `research/`.
- Planning frameworks in `plans/`.
- Project constraints in `AGENTS.md` and `docs/00_project_context.md`.

## Workflow

1. Save the original research text in `research/raw/`.
2. Create a checked working summary in `research/`.
3. Separate:
   - strong evidence.
   - mixed evidence.
   - limited evidence.
   - coaching inference.
4. Extract:
   - key metrics.
   - planning rules.
   - risk warnings.
   - open questions.
5. Update relevant files in `plans/` or `templates/`.
6. Keep the output practical for a 2x/week amateur/semi-pro rugby setting.

## Required Checks

- Does this apply to U22/development rugby?
- Does it avoid copying professional volume?
- Does it account for work, study, sleep, stress, and variable attendance?
- Does it distinguish rugby evidence from general team-sport inference?
- Does it respect medical boundaries?

## Output Pattern

- raw file.
- reviewed research file.
- direct consequences for annual planning.
- open questions.
