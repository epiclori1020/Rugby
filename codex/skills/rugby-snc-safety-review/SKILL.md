# Rugby S&C Safety Review

## Goal

Find problems before a plan reaches the field.

## Review Checklist

1. Load spikes:
   - Are missed sessions being "made up"?
   - Is Tuesday or Thursday overloaded?
   - Is conditioning added after heavy lower-body work without reason?

2. Contact load:
   - Is contact planned separately?
   - Is full contact limited?
   - Are controlled contact and set-piece exposures clear?

3. Speed/COD:
   - Is speed exposure gradual?
   - Are hamstring/calf/adductor risks considered?
   - Are COD and deceleration volumes appropriate?

4. Returners:
   - Are Speed, COD, Conditioning, and Contact capped separately?
   - Is next-morning response checked?
   - Is medical/physio status clear?

5. Concussion:
   - Any suspected concussion means remove and refer.
   - No same-day return.
   - S&C never clears return to play.

6. Amateur/semi-pro feasibility:
   - Does it account for work, study, sleep and stress?
   - Is monitoring short?
   - Is the plan executable with small gym capacity?

## Output Pattern

- Pass/fail summary.
- Highest-risk issues.
- Specific edits.
- What can stay unchanged.
- Open questions for coach/physio/club.
